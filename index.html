About Me

Nice to meet you! my name is Mikaal and I am currently 21 years old. I was born in Houston, Texas but my background is Pakistani. Although I was born in Houston and my ethinicity is Pakistani, I've lived more so in Dubai and Mississauga. My hobbies include watching soccer (football) and cricket, playing video games, going to the gym, and watching TV.

In terms of studies, I'm an undergraduate at the University of Toronto Mississauga persuing TCS, CCIT, and Economics, with a passion for building meaningful, user-centred experiences. I've also studied micro and macroeconomics, giving me insight into how users, systems, and incentives interact. Always curious, I enjoy working on projects at the intersection of design, AI, and human behaviour.
